Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

Side-Label Symmetry
Audit that swaps FOR/AGAINST labels to detect evaluator bias. Small ΔD indicates fair evaluation.
Extraction Stability
Consistency of FACT/ARGUMENT extraction across pipeline reruns. Measured via Jaccard overlap.
Topic Drift
Semantic change in topic scope from previous snapshots. High drift may indicate topic instability.
Relevance Sensitivity
How much the verdict changes when topic relevance weights are perturbed. Tests robustness to content mass variations.
IQR
Interquartile Range — spread of scores across judges or replicates.
