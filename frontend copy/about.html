Blind LLM-Adjudicated Debate System

Canonical Design Specification (abridged)

Core Principles

Blindness

  • No usernames / pseudonyms
  • No profiles
  • No likes / reactions
  • No contribution counts
  • No team identities

Visible Modulation

  • Moderation rules are visible
  • Templates are versioned
  • Only Allowed posts influence scoring
  • Block reasons are published

Pipeline Overview

  1. Posts → Modulation (allow/block)
  2. Allowed posts → Topic Consolidation (bounded, neutral framing)
  3. Spans → Argument Unit Construction (Facts → Inference)
  4. FACT Extraction → Canonicalization (dedupe)
  5. ARGUMENT Canonicalization → Deduplication
  6. Side Summaries → Steelman FROM canonical arguments
  7. Fact-Check → P(fact true) assignment
  8. Topic Scoring → Multi-judge (F, Reason, Cov)
  9. Debate Aggregation → Topic-weighted
  10. Verdict → Statistical Separability across replicates
  11. Confidence → Replicate Stability

Scoring Formula Reference

Factuality

F_{t,s} = (1/K_{t,s}) × Σ_k p_{t,s,k}

Mean fact-check probability across claims for topic t and side s.

Reasoning

Reason_{t,s,a} = median_j(Reason_{t,s,a,j})

Reason_{t,s} = median_a(Reason_{t,s,a})

Judge medians per argument, then median across arguments.

Coverage

Cov_{t,s} = Σ(addressed) Lev_{t,s̄,a} / Σ(all) Lev_{t,s̄,a}

Share of the opposing side's argument load that is directly addressed.

Quality

Q_{t,s} = (F_{t,s} × Reason_{t,s} × Cov_{t,s})^{1/3}

Geometric mean that rewards balance across factuality, reasoning, and coverage.

Overall

Overall_s = Σ_t (Rel_t × Q_{t,s})

Topic relevance-weighted quality sum for side s.

Margin

D = Overall_FOR - Overall_AGAINST

Positive D favors FOR, negative D favors AGAINST.

Verdict

If CI(D) entirely > 0: FOR wins

If CI(D) entirely < 0: AGAINST wins

Else: NO VERDICT

Key Design Decisions

  • Geometric mean for Q: Enforces balance without tuned weights
  • Deduplication: Repetition does not increase scores
  • Median aggregation: Robust against outlier judges
  • Replicate-based verdict: Conservative; only commit when stable
  • Traceability: Every fact and argument links to source spans
