Primary Admin Rhythm

The policy workspace is for shaping the next moderation version. The other workspaces are for reviewing requests and checking whether the live outcomes support the policy you are about to publish.

Existing snapshots remain immutable. Any policy version you apply here affects future snapshots only, and every published change remains visible in the audit trail.

Recommended Flow

  1. Use Policy Workspace to start from the active template, adjust guardrails, then review the participant-facing summary.
  2. Use Proposal Queue only when you are triaging submitted debates.
  3. Use Snapshot Audit after you draft policy changes, not before, to confirm the live distribution supports them.
  4. Use Frame Governance as a separate governance lane, not as part of routine moderation tuning.
