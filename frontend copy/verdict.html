Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

D (Margin)
Difference between the overall FOR and AGAINST scores. Positive means FOR leads; negative means AGAINST leads.
CI(D)
Confidence interval around the margin D. If it crosses zero, the system keeps the verdict at NO VERDICT.
Topic contribution
How much a topic's quality gap, weighted by relevance, moves the overall margin.
