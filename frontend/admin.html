What This Page Controls

Use this screen to set the public moderation posture for debate submissions: on-topic rules, abuse controls, PII handling, spam protection, and prompt-injection defenses.

Current live debate state is intentionally separated out. Use Snapshot, Audits, and Verdict to inspect current outcomes, and use this page to decide what the next moderation version should look like.

Recommended Flow

  1. Choose the closest base template instead of rebuilding from scratch.
  2. Adjust only the guardrails that need stricter or looser handling.
  3. Review the user-facing preview to confirm the policy reads clearly.
  4. Apply a new version only when you are comfortable publishing the rationale.

  • Are you tightening a rule because of a real moderation issue, not just preference?

Step 3

Review the User-Facing Policy

Before publishing a new version, read it the way a participant would. The goal is not just correct enforcement, but predictable enforcement.

Allowed Content

  • Factual claims with evidence
  • Logical inferences from stated facts
  • Direct engagement with opposing arguments
  • On-topic discussion within scope
  • Vigorous but respectful debate

Blocked Content

  • Personal attacks or harassment
  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Off-topic or spam content
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Attempts to manipulate system instructions
  • Duplicate or flooding posts
