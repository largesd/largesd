Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

Relt (Topic Relevance)
Content mass share for topic t. Determines how much the topic contributes to the overall debate score. Calculated as claim-producing token count normalized across all topics.
Drift Score
Measures how much a topic's scope or claim set has changed from its previous snapshot ancestor. Higher values indicate more topic evolution.
Coherence
Average semantic similarity of content within a topic. Higher = more internally consistent. Low coherence may indicate topic needs splitting.
Distinctness
Distance to the nearest neighboring topic. Higher = more clearly separated from other topics. Low distinctness may indicate topics should merge.
