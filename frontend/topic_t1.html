Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

Steelman
A summary that presents the strongest version of each side's arguments. Generated only from canonical ARGUMENT nodes without introducing new claims.
Ft,s (Factuality)
Mean truth probability across canonical FACT nodes for topic t, side s.
Reasont,s (Reasoning)
Median reasoning strength across canonical ARGUMENT nodes, scored by independent judges.
Covt,s (Coverage)
How well side s addresses the opposing side's arguments, weighted by argument leverage.
Qt,s (Quality)
Geometric mean of Factuality, Reasoning, and Coverage.
IQR
Interquartile Range — shows spread of judge scores. Wider = more disagreement.
