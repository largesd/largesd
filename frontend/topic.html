Glossary & Help

Topic geometry metrics and terms.

Relevance (Rel)
Content mass share - how much of the total debate content belongs to this topic.
Drift Score
How much this topic has changed from its previous state. Higher = more change.
Coherence
How internally consistent the arguments in this topic are.
Distinctness
How different this topic is from other topics (prevents duplication).
Steelman
A summary presenting the strongest version of each side's arguments.
