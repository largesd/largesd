Glossary & Help

Key terms used in evidence-target analysis and robustness audits.

Impact on D
Estimated movement in the verdict margin if the target changes substantially.
Update trigger
A threshold statement describing what evidence would materially change the verdict.
Side-label symmetry
Audit that swaps FOR and AGAINST labels to detect evaluator bias. Smaller movement in D means better symmetry.
Extraction stability
Consistency of FACT and ARGUMENT extraction across reruns of the pipeline.
Relevance sensitivity
How much the verdict changes when topic relevance weights are perturbed.
