Blind LLM-Adjudicated Debate System

Canonical Design Specification (abridged)

Core Principles

Blindness

  • No usernames / pseudonyms
  • No profiles
  • No likes / reactions
  • No contribution counts
  • No team identities

Visible Modulation

  • Moderation rules are visible
  • Templates are versioned
  • Only Allowed posts influence scoring
  • Block reasons are published

Pipeline Overview

  1. Posts → Modulation (allow/block)
  2. Allowed posts → Topic Consolidation (bounded, neutral framing)
  3. Spans → Argument Unit Construction (Facts → Inference)
  4. FACT Extraction → Canonicalization (dedupe)
  5. ARGUMENT Canonicalization → Deduplication
  6. Side Summaries → Steelman FROM canonical arguments
  7. Fact-Check → P(fact true) assignment
  8. Topic Scoring → Multi-judge (F, Reason, Cov)
  9. Debate Aggregation → Topic-weighted
  10. Verdict → Statistical Separability across replicates
  11. Confidence → Replicate Stability

Scoring Formula Reference

Factuality: F_{t,s} = (1/K_{t,s}) × Σ_k p_{t,s,k} Reasoning: Reason_{t,s,a} = median_j(Reason_{t,s,a,j}) Reason_{t,s} = median_a(Reason_{t,s,a}) Coverage: Cov_{t,s} = Σ(addressed) Lev_{t,s̄,a} / Σ(all) Lev_{t,s̄,a} Quality: Q_{t,s} = (F_{t,s} × Reason_{t,s} × Cov_{t,s})^{1/3} Overall: Overall_s = Σ_t (Rel_t × Q_{t,s}) Margin: D = Overall_FOR − Overall_AGAINST Verdict: If CI(D) entirely > 0: FOR wins If CI(D) entirely < 0: AGAINST wins Else: NO VERDICT

Key Design Decisions

  • Geometric mean for Q: Enforces balance without tuned weights
  • Deduplication: Repetition does not increase scores
  • Median aggregation: Robust against outlier judges
  • Replicate-based verdict: Conservative; only commit when stable
  • Traceability: Every fact and argument links to source spans

Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

F_{t,s}
Factuality score for topic t, side s. Mean of P(true) across canonical facts.
Reason_{t,s}
Reasoning strength for topic t, side s. Median across canonical arguments.
Cov_{t,s}
Coverage score for topic t, side s. Proportion of opposing arguments addressed.
Q_{t,s}
Quality score for topic t, side s. Geometric mean of F, Reason, Cov.
CI(D)
Confidence Interval for the margin D. Verdict depends on whether this crosses zero.

For full specification, see design document.
