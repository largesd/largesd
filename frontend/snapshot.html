    Glossary & Help

    Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

    Snapshot
    An immutable capture of the debate state at a point in time. Includes the current verdict, moderation outcomes, topics, and scores.
    Trigger
    What caused the snapshot to be created: activity-based, scheduled, or manual.
    Confidence Δ
    How much the confidence changed between the latest snapshot and the immediately previous one.
    Diff Surface
    The concrete items that changed between two snapshots: posts, topics, facts, arguments, and scores.

    For full specification, see About page.
