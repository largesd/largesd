Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

Canonical ARGUMENT
A structured argument unit with supporting canonical facts and an inference/conclusion. Deduplicated across similar argument structures.
Reason (Reasoning)
Median strength of the inference from facts to conclusion, as scored by independent judges (0-1).
Coverage
Proportion of opposing arguments that are addressed (rebutted, qualified, or conceded), weighted by their decisiveness.
IQR
Interquartile Range of judge scores. Higher values indicate more disagreement among evaluators.
Structure Overlap
Stability metric showing how consistently argument structures are extracted across pipeline reruns.
