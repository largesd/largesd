Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

Snapshot
An immutable capture of the debate state at a point in time. Includes all posts, topics, facts, arguments, and scores.
Trigger
What caused the snapshot to be created: activity-based (new posts), time-based (scheduled), or manual.
Mixed Stance
A post that contributes spans to both FOR and AGAINST within the same topic. Indicates nuanced argumentation.
Diff Surface
Compare layer showing exactly what changed between two snapshots (posts, topics, facts, arguments, scores).
