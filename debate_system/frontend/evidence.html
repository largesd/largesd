Glossary & Help

Key terms and metrics used throughout the system.

|p−0.5| (Decisiveness)
Distance of P(true) from uncertainty (0.5). Higher values = more decisive facts. Facts near 0.5 are best targets for new evidence.
Leverage
How much an argument influences the overall outcome, based on the decisiveness of its supporting facts.
Update Trigger
Specific threshold where new evidence would change the verdict or topic-level outcome.
